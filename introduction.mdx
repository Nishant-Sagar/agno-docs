sidebarTitle: Introduction

---

Agno is a multi-agent framework that helps you run private and secure agent systems in your cloud. It brings you speed, performance and reliability with a delightful developer experience.

**Agno provides:**

1. The most advanced framework for building agents, multi-agent teams and agentic workflows.
2. A horizontally scalable FastAPI server for running your agents, teams and workflows in production called the AgentOS.
3. A powerful UI for testing, monitoring and managing your agent system. No data ever leaves your system.

**Using Agno, you can build a complete agentic solution that runs securely inside your cloud.** It is a novel architecture that no other framework provides, you get the most advanced agent framework, an API for running in production and a control plane for managing your production system. You also get complete data privacy and control, your AgentOS runs in your cloud and no data ever leaves your system.
